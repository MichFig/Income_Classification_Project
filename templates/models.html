About the Machine Learning Models

In order see how well someone's income was determined/predicted by the fields from the dataset, we looked at three different models and used their final accuracy. This final accuracy of each model also let us determine which model was the most predictive, and use this on the predict page to make predictions!

Multivariate Linear Regression

As expected, didn't perform well with categorical data, model score is only 0.22.

Decision Tree and Random Forest

Performed better than linear. Due to the many choices for each input, scores are .786 and .816.

Neural Network

Best performing model with an accuracy score of .837!
